Lentil Soup

Description

It is a useful dish, easy to cook even for the uninitiated, heathy and lastly tasteful. Although the lentil is on the not so notorious side of the food spectrum, nonetheless it can be compared to others such as beans.

Given that it is made mainly from vegetables, its lack of meat show a healty balance for the more calories full meal served usually.

Ingredients

  • lentil 250g
  • onion 1 medium/big piece
  • garlic 5 cloves
  • tomato sauce 400 ml

Steps

  1. cook the onion and garlic for 5 minutes
  2. add spieces (at your discretion)
  3. add the lentil
  4. add 500 ml of whater
  5. let it cook for about 30-40 minutes
